Arkveld
Arkveld is a large quadruped monster covered in grayish white scales, white fur and armor plating. It has ram-like horns on each side of its head, a thick armored tail, a large plated lower jaw and a mouth full of sharp teeth.

Rey Dau
Rey Dau is a large bipedal wyvern covered in bony golden scales, as well as smaller black scales on its underbelly, joints, and wings. Unlike most other bipedal Flying Wyverns, its wings are not comprised of extended digits, instead being a single membrane similar to that of pterosaurs.
